UX Architect, Front-End Engineer

Expert web and mobile user interface designer; end-to-end user experience and product designer; front-end engineer; and brand development expert. Most recently, led a team of designers at LevelUp in the creation of highly regarded mobile- and web-based applications.

Portfolio:

LevelUp

LevelUp is an app that allows consumers to pay with their phone at thousands of locations in the United States. With over 1M registered users executing upwards of 12,000 transactions per day totalling just under $1M dollars in sales per week, and a nearly 5-star overall rating in the App Store, LevelUp has emerged as an industry leader, often praised for its ease-of-use. I led the design team and oversaw all aspects of design for LevelUp, including: mobile and web interfaces for consumers, businesses and internal teams — from wireframes to pixel-perfect Photoshop mockups; hardware design and prototyping using SketchUp and KeyShot; branding and logo development; print, digital and email marketing; and front-end engineering using HAML, SCSS and JavaScript in a Ruby on Rails environment with Git for version control.

— Typical App Store Review
